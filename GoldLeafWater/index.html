Oak Ridges Moraine, our spring water source is under the protection of Conservation Act, ensuring its ongoing ecological and hydrological integrity.

Our bodies need a balanced ph. Goldleaf, with the perfectly balanced ph of 7.35, helps moderate our body ph.

The pure, clean taste of goldleaf water comes from the naturally-occurring combination of minerals. However, minerals do not just add to its taste, they have a huge impact on one's health.

Goldleaf's bottling facility is just a few hundred feet from water source. To ensure consistent high quality, we perform daily in-house testing of our well and throughout the bottling and packing process.
