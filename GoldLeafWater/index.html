Our premium, Canadian 100% natural spring water source is located on a protected site in the Oak Ridges Moraine approximately 115 km northeast of Toronto. A never-ending supply of naturally-filtered, pure Canadian spring water Under the Oak Ridges Moraine Conservation Act, our source and the surrounding area are protected, ensuring its ongoing ecological and hydrological integrity. This means our pure, natural spring water will continue to be the highest quality.

At a pH of 7.35 ppm, our water is perfectly balanced.

The pure, clean taste of our water comes from the naturally-occurring combination of minerals. However, minerals do not just add to its taste, they have a huge impact on one’s health and also the water’s pH level.

Guaranteed Quality

Bottled at source

Our spring water originates just a few hundred feet from the bottling facility.

Tests

To ensure consistent high quality, we perform daily in-house testing of our well and throughout the bottling and packing process.

Outside lab tests are also performed on a monthly basis (E. coli, HPC, Total Coliform).

The surrounding environment is also regularly monitored to ensure the ongoing purity of our water supply.

Detection

With respect to regulations, in Canada bottled water is regulated under the Food and Drugs Act and Regulations as a food product.
